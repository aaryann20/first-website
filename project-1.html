GYM EQUPIMENTS AVAILABLE

1. Treadmill Treadmill Treadmill is one of the perfect gym machines for ladies. Running on the treadmill is a great way to get the desired shape. As it targets your hips, glutes, leg muscles and strengthens the calf muscles, hamstrings and quadriceps.

2. Stationary Bike Stationary Bike Stationary bike is a perfect way to burn those serious calories. Instead of spending your money in a local gym opt for a stationary bike, the ideal gym equipment for women that is super cost-efficient. You can use it anytime. Nowadays home workout videos are available online so you can use the workout app for guidance.

3. Total Gym Core Trainer Don’t get confused with this complicated name this machine is very simple and easy to use. It comes with a rolling glide board with handles and pad at the top. This ladies gym equipment is specially designed for core exercise. It recruits back, abdominals, shoulder and hips to work in unison for better stability and balance.

4. Water Rower WaterRower provides a complete body workout. If your fitness goal is to slim down then this women’s fitness equipment is for you. This machine includes lower body pushing with upper body pulling. It also benefits your lungs and heart.

5. Smith Machine This exercise equipment for women is an alternative to barbells and free weights and consists of a vertical bar fixed within steel rails. You can easily perform muscle building exercises such as shoulder presses, squats and deadlifts. This is a user-friendly machine and is perfect for novice weightlifters.
